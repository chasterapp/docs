Find other Chaster members based on their profile, interests, and activity. Member search helps you discover people who share your kinks, own similar toys, or match other criteria you care about.

## Searching for members

Navigate to the **Members** page to browse the community. Results appear as profile cards showing each member's avatar, username, role, badges, and a short description. More results load as you scroll down the page.

## Filters

Open the filter drawer to narrow your search. You can combine as many filters as you want.

### Age

Filter by age range using a slider.

### Location

Filter by country and region.

### Role

Show only wearers, only keyholders, or both.

### Gender

Filter by gender identity.

### Sexual orientation

Filter by sexual orientation.

### Kinks

Select specific kinks to filter by. By default, results include members who have rated **any** of your selected kinks. Enable **Match all kinks** to only show members who have rated every kink you selected.

### Toys

Select specific toys to filter by. Like kinks, you can toggle **Match all toys** to require all selected toys instead of any.

### Financial interests

Filter members by their financial domination status. You can choose to show only members with financial interests, or exclude them from results.

### Activity

Enable **Active in the last 7 days** to show only recently active members.

:::tip
Your search filters are saved automatically, so you see the same results when you come back to the Members page.
:::
