Add photos to your profile so other members can see who you are. Pictures go through a moderation review before appearing publicly, and you can optionally verify them through a liveness check.

:::info
Profile pictures require a [Chaster Plus](/chaster-plus) subscription.
:::

## Uploading pictures

You can upload up to 9 photos to your profile. To manage your pictures, go to **Settings > Kink Profile > Pictures**.

Select one or more photos (up to 5 per upload), then submit them for review. You can track the status of each picture directly from your settings.

## Moderation review

All pictures are reviewed by a moderator before they appear on your profile. Each picture has one of three statuses:

- **Pending**: Waiting for moderator review.
- **Accepted**: Approved and visible on your profile.
- **Rejected**: Declined by a moderator.

If a picture is rejected, you will see a reason. You can delete the rejected picture and upload a replacement.

## Liveness verification

When uploading pictures, you can optionally complete a liveness verification to prove you are the person in the photos. This is not required, but verified pictures display a green **Verified** badge on your profile, which helps build trust with other members.

### How it works

1. Start the liveness verification during your upload.
2. The system prompts you with a random action to perform (for example, a thumbs up, a head turn, or a peace sign).
3. Record a short video of yourself completing the action.
4. Submit the video along with your pictures.

A moderator reviews the video alongside your photos. If the verification passes, your pictures receive the Verified badge.

### Saving your verification

You can save your liveness verification so you do not need to record a new video each time you upload pictures. Your saved verification is reused automatically on future uploads. You can delete a saved verification at any time from your settings.
