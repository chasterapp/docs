## What is a Progressive Web App?

Chaster is a Progressive Web App (PWA). A PWA is a type of website that can be installed directly on your device, giving you quick access from your home screen without needing to download anything from an app store. When installed, Chaster looks and behaves like a native application.

## Why install Chaster?

Installing the PWA on your device provides several benefits over using it in a browser:

- **Push notifications**: Receive alerts for lock events, messages, and other activity. On iOS, push notifications are only available when the PWA is installed.
- **Full-screen experience**: Chaster runs without the browser toolbar, giving you more screen space.
- **Quick access**: Launch Chaster with a single tap from your home screen, just like any other app.
- **Dedicated app switcher entry**: Chaster appears as its own app when switching between applications.

:::tip
On iOS, you **must** install the PWA to receive push notifications. This is an operating system limitation that does not apply to Android.
:::

## iOS

### Using Safari

:::caution
On iOS, PWAs can only be installed through Safari. Other browsers such as Chrome and Firefox do not support this feature.
:::

1. Open [chaster.app](https://chaster.app) in Safari, and open the menu.

<img src="/img/install-ios-safari-open.png" alt="Open chaster.app in Safari" className="img-mobile" />

2. Tap the **Share** icon (the square with an upward arrow) in the toolbar.

<img src="/img/install-ios-safari-share.png" alt="Tap the Share icon in the Safari toolbar" className="img-mobile" />

3. In the share sheet, tap **More** (`...`) to reveal additional options.

<img src="/img/install-ios-safari-more.png" alt="Tap More in the share sheet" className="img-mobile" />

4. Tap **Add to Home Screen**.

<img src="/img/install-ios-safari-add-to-home.png" alt="Tap Add to Home Screen" className="img-mobile" />

5. Optionally rename the shortcut, then tap **Add**.

<img src="/img/install-ios-safari-confirm.png" alt="Confirm by tapping Add" className="img-mobile" />

Chaster will now appear on your home screen.

<img src="/img/install-ios-home-screen.png" alt="Chaster icon on the iOS home screen" className="img-mobile" />

## Android

### Using Chrome

1. Open [chaster.app](https://chaster.app) in Chrome.

<img src="/img/install-android-chrome-open.png" alt="Open chaster.app in Chrome" className="img-mobile" />

2. A banner may appear at the bottom of the screen prompting you to **Add Chaster to Home screen**. Tap it to proceed.
3. If no banner appears, tap the menu icon (**⋮**) in the top-right corner and select **Add to Home screen**.

<img src="/img/install-android-chrome-menu.png" alt="Add to Home screen option in the Chrome menu" className="img-mobile" />

4. Confirm by tapping **Install** in the dialog.

<img src="/img/install-android-chrome-confirm.png" alt="Confirm by tapping Install" className="img-mobile" />

5. On the next screen, tap **Add to home screen** to place the Chaster icon on your home screen.

<img src="/img/install-android-chrome-add-to-home.png" alt="Tap Add to Home Screen" className="img-mobile" />

Chaster will now appear on your home screen.

<img src="/img/install-android-home-screen.png" alt="Chaster icon on the Android home screen" className="img-mobile" />

:::tip
Installation also works in other Chromium-based browsers such as Samsung Internet, Brave, and Edge.
:::

## Troubleshooting

### The install banner does not appear on Android

Chrome only shows the install banner once. If you previously dismissed it, tap the menu icon (**⋮**) and select **Add to Home screen** instead.

### I don't see "Add to Home Screen" on iOS

Make sure you are using Safari. On iOS, other browsers do not support PWA installation. Also verify that you are tapping the **Share** icon (not the bookmark icon).

### How to uninstall

To remove Chaster from your home screen, long-press the icon and select **Remove** or **Delete**. This does not delete your account or any data, you can reinstall it at any time.
