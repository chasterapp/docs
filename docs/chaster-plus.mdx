With [Chaster Plus](https://chaster.app/plus), access to endless possibilities of customization, with exclusive extensions and games.

## What is Chaster Plus ?

Chaster Plus is a monthly subscription allowing preview access to new extensions, as well as exclusive features, for an ultimate experience.

By subscribing, you also support the Chaster developers, who work in their spare time to create the ultimate online chastity experience.

## What's included in Chaster Plus

By being a Plus member, you will have access to new and unique features.

### Unlimited locks and extensions

You can create as many locks as you want. Normal users can only have one lock locked at a time, unlike Plus members who can create unlimited locks at the same time: useful for chastity lock, bondage lock, or other activities.

Normal users are also limited in the number of extensions: they are limited to three extensions, unlike Plus members who can add as many as they wish.

### Exclusive extensions and adventures

Every month we develop new features and extensions for users. By being a Plus member, you will have access to these functionalities before the others, and you will be able to test the newly developed extensions in advance.

### Image verification

When you take a picture of your combination, it may be blurred, and prevent you from seeing the code at the end of the session. If you are afraid that you will not be able to free yourself at the end of the session for this reason, you can request a verification of your combination image.

You can request a verification when you create the session by checking the associated checkbox. Once the session has started, don't close your lock box right away, but wait for a moderator to confirm the picture.

If your image is validated by a moderator and the combination is readable, you will receive an e-mail and a notification. If it is not the case, your lock will be automatically unlocked and you will be able to recreate a new session.

### Create templates

You can import a shared lock that you have created to create your own session. This will import the initial time, as well as all the parameters of the lock and the configuration of the extensions.
