Chaster tracks your activity as a wearer and keyholder with detailed statistics. You can review your lock history, streaks, and patterns over different time periods.

## Where to find your statistics

Your statistics appear in two places:

- **Profile > Statistics tab**: Shows both wearer and keyholder statistics. Switch between the two using the sub-tabs at the top.
- **Calendar page**: Shows your wearer statistics in a side panel next to the calendar.

Your profile overview also displays a 3-month activity heatmap in the sidebar. Click the heatmap to go to your full statistics.

## Time periods

You can filter your activity statistics by time period using the dropdown selector:

- **Last week**: the past 7 days
- **Last month**: the past 30 days
- **Last 3 months**: the past 90 days (this is the default)
- **Last 6 months**: the past 180 days
- **Last year**: the past 365 days
- **All time**: your entire history on Chaster

The period filter applies to the activity section. Streak and global statistics always cover your full history.

## Wearer statistics

Wearer statistics measure your lock activity as someone wearing a chastity device.

### Streak

- **Current streak**: how long you have been continuously locked, starting from your current active lock. If you are not currently locked, this is not shown.
- **Longest streak**: the longest continuous period you have ever been locked.

Streaks are always all-time values and are not affected by the period filter.

### Activity

Activity statistics cover the selected time period:

- **Consistency**: the percentage of the selected period you spent locked. If you were locked for 20 out of 30 days, your consistency is about 67%.
- **Best period**: your longest continuous lock duration within the selected period.
- **Purity**: the percentage of days without an orgasm during the selected period. If you went 27 out of 30 days without an orgasm, your purity is 90%.
- **Best denial**: the longest stretch of consecutive days without an orgasm during the selected period.
- **Shortest lock duration**: the duration of your shortest completed lock in the period.
- **Longest lock duration**: the duration of your longest completed lock in the period.
- **Average lock duration**: the average duration across all completed locks in the period.
- **Completed locks**: the number of locks that ended normally during the period.
- **Deserted locks**: the number of locks you abandoned during the period.
- **Orgasm count**: the total number of orgasm events you logged during the period.

Purity, best denial, and orgasm count only appear if you have logged at least one orgasm event in your [calendar](/features/calendar). These statistics always start from your first orgasm event, regardless of the selected period. For example, if the period is set to 3 months but your first orgasm was logged 10 days ago, purity and best denial are calculated over those 10 days only.

Lock duration statistics (shortest, longest, average) are computed from locks that ended during the selected period, not locks that were active during it.

### Global

Global statistics always cover your entire history:

- **Longest session**: the duration of your longest single lock session ever.
- **Total time locked**: your cumulative time spent locked across all sessions. If you had overlapping locks, the overlapping time is only counted once.

:::info
Self-reported lock periods from the [calendar](/features/calendar) count toward your wearer statistics alongside verified Chaster locks.
:::

## Keyholder statistics

Keyholder statistics measure your activity as someone managing locks for other users.

### Currently locked

Shows the wearers you are currently managing, along with their avatars and how long each has been locked.

### Activity

Activity statistics cover the selected time period:

- **Active locks**: the number of locks currently active that you are managing.
- **Locks completed**: the number of locks that your wearers completed during the period.
- **Unique wearers**: the number of distinct users you managed during the period.
- **New locks**: the number of locks started under your management during the period.
- **Shortest lock duration**: the duration of the shortest completed lock you managed in the period.
- **Longest lock duration**: the duration of the longest completed lock you managed in the period.
- **Average lock duration**: the average duration across all completed locks you managed in the period.
- **Shared locks count**: the number of shared locks associated with your keyholder activity.

### Global

Global statistics always cover your entire history:

- **Total locks managed**: the total number of locks you have managed as a keyholder.
- **Total time keyholding**: the cumulative duration of all locks you have managed.

## Privacy

You can control whether other users see your statistics. Go to **Settings > Privacy** and toggle **Show my statistics publicly**. When enabled, anyone visiting your profile can see your Statistics tab, your activity heatmap, and the calendar on your overview. When disabled, only you can see your Statistics tab.

## Resetting statistics

You can reset your statistics to start tracking from a new date. This is useful if you want a fresh start without your earlier lock history affecting your numbers.

To reset your statistics:

1. Go to your **Profile > Statistics tab** and open the menu, or go to the **Calendar** statistics panel and open the menu.
2. Select the reset option for the role you want to reset (wearer, keyholder, or both).
3. Confirm and click **Reset** to apply.

Resetting sets a new start date. All locks from before that date are excluded from your statistics calculations. On the calendar, verified locks from before the reset date no longer appear.

Resetting your statistics also affects your [achievements](/profile/achievements). Time-based achievements are recalculated from the new start date, and achievements you previously earned may be revoked if your post-reset stats no longer meet the required thresholds.

:::caution
Resetting statistics is permanent and cannot be undone. Related achievements will also be reset.
:::
