Track your lock history and orgasms over time with the calendar. The calendar combines events from your Chaster locks with manually recorded entries, giving you a visual timeline of your chastity journey.

To access the calendar, go to the **Calendar** page from the main navigation.

## Lock period events

Lock period events represent time spent locked. They appear as spans on the calendar. There are two kinds: verified and self-reported.

### Verified lock periods

Verified lock periods are automatically synced from your Chaster locks. They show the lock title, keyholder name, and duration. Because they come directly from your lock data, they are read-only and cannot be edited or deleted.

### Self-reported lock periods

Self-reported lock periods let you record past lock sessions that were not tracked on Chaster. You can edit or delete them at any time.

Each self-reported lock period has the following fields:

- **Start date**: When the lock period began.
- **End date**: When the lock period ended. Leave this blank and enable "Is ongoing" if you are still locked.
- **All-day**: Toggle to record the event as a full-day entry without specific times.
- **Is ongoing**: Toggle to indicate a lock period that has not ended yet.
- **Description**: Optional notes about the lock period, up to 1,000 characters.

### Adding a lock period

1. Click the **Add event** dropdown menu and select **Lock period**.
2. Set the start date and, if applicable, the end date.
3. Optionally toggle "All-day" or "Is ongoing."
4. Optionally add a description.
5. Click **Save** to create the event.

## Orgasm events

Orgasm events record individual orgasms as point-in-time entries on the calendar. The date must be in the past.

### Orgasm details

Each orgasm event has the following fields:

- **Date**: When the orgasm occurred. Must be a past date.
- **Orgasm type**: Select one or more types: normal, ruined, forced, prostate-only, non-ejaculatory, supervised, or wet dream.
- **Stimulation method**: Select one or more methods: masturbation, oral, penetration, hands-free, or toys.
- **Description**: Optional notes, up to 1,000 characters.

### Techniques

You can optionally mark an orgasm event with one or both techniques:

- **Edging**: Record the orgasm as an edging session and specify the number of edges (1 to 999).
- **Speed challenge**: Record the orgasm as a speed challenge and specify the duration in minutes and seconds.

### Adding an orgasm event

1. Click the **Add event** dropdown menu and select **Orgasm**.
2. Set the date and time.
3. Select one or more orgasm types and stimulation methods.
4. Optionally enable edging or speed challenge and fill in the details.
5. Optionally add a description.
6. Click **Save** to create the event.

## Calendar views

### Month view

The month view is the default. It shows a traditional month grid where each day is visually marked as locked or unlocked based on your lock period events. Click any event to see its details in a popover.

### Year view

The year view shows all 12 months at a glance. It is useful for spotting long-term patterns in your lock history.

### Navigation

- Use the **previous** and **next** buttons to move between periods.
- Click **Today** to return to the current date.
- Use the view switcher to toggle between month and year views.

A legend below the calendar toolbar explains the visual indicators for locked and unlocked days.

## Statistics

The statistics panel appears alongside the calendar and summarizes your wearer activity over a selected time period. It shows stats like your consistency, best lock period, lock durations, and more. Use the period selector to choose the time range (for example, the last 3 months).

For a full breakdown of each statistic, see [Statistics](/features/statistics).

You can reset your wearer statistics from the menu in the statistics panel. Resetting sets a new start date: all locks from before that date are excluded from your statistics, and verified lock periods from before the reset date no longer appear on the calendar. See [Resetting statistics](/features/statistics#resetting-statistics) for details.

## Editing and deleting events

Self-reported lock periods and orgasm events can be edited or deleted at any time. Click on the event to open its details, then select **Edit** or **Delete** from the menu.

Verified lock periods synced from your Chaster locks are read-only.
