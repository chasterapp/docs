## Introduction

Chaster is a keyholding application for chastity wearers and keyholders. With Chaster, you can have exciting adventures with custom time-based locks. Lock yourself in chastity in one of thousands of locks created by the community, or create your own custom lock.

## Overview

Chaster works with a physical key lockbox, a small box with a resettable numeric code. Here's the basic flow:

1. Lock your chastity key inside the lockbox with a combination you don't know.
2. Upload a photo of the combination to Chaster (without looking at it yourself).
3. Start your session. Chaster keeps the combination secret until the timer ends.

When your session is over, Chaster reveals the code so you can open the lockbox and retrieve your key. Learn more in the [Your first lock](/your-first-lock) guide.

## Extensions

One key feature of Chaster is the extension system. Extensions let you configure your locks with an infinite number of possibilities. You can combine multiple extensions to customize your session and create your own chastity experience.
